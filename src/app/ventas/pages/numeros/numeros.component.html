Decimalpipe y porcentajes

Pipes incluidos en Angular (Common Module).

  • "ventasNetas | number"
  • "ventasNetas | number:'1.2-2'"
  • "ventasNetas | number:'1.4-4'"
  • "ventasNetas | number:'1.4-4':'fr'"
  • {{ventasNetas | number}}
  • {{ventasNetas | number:'1.2-2'}}
  • {{ventasNetas | number:'1.4-4'}}
  • {{ventasNetas | number:'1.4-4':'fr'}}
  • "ventasNetas + 2000000 | currency:'CAD':'symbol-narrow':'1.0-4'"
  • {{ventasNetas + 2000000 | currency:'CAD':'symbol-narrow':'1.0-4'}}
  • "porcentaje | percent"
  • "porcentaje | percent:'2.2-2"
  • {{porcentaje | percent}}
  • {{porcentaje | percent:'2.2-2'}}
